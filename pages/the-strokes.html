The Strokes

My second favorite band. I love traveling and listening to them.

The Strokes are an American rock band from New York City formed in 1998. They were a leading group of the early-2000s indie rock revival.

The band is composed of lead singer and songwriter Julian Casablancas, guitarists Nick Valensi and Albert Hammond Jr., bassist Nikolai Fraiture, and drummer Fabrizio Moretti.

My favorite songs are:

  • You Only Live Once
  • Reptilia
  • Last Nite
