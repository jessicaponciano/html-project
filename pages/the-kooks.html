The Kooks

This band is definitely my favorite band and I love listening to their music in the morning.

The Kooks are an English pop-rock band formed in 2004 in Brighton. The band consists of Luke Pritchard, Hugh Harris and Alexis Nunez.

Their music is primarily influenced by the 1960s British Invasion movement and post-punk revival of the new millennium.

My favorite songs are:

  • Naive
  • Seaside
  • Sway
